No Ideas to Big Ideas

And Ideas that are improbable in 10+ hours.




At the very beginning, we had an idea of making a game similiar to T-Rex Run; an endless run and jump game. If you don't know T-Rex Run, it's a google chrome game that pops up when there's no interent or wifi. However, we don't where to start or how to make one, and one of our team members suggested a RPG game. That's where we also found RPG Maker MV to make our game. Eventhough our creation is not what we had expected, it definitely took us by surprised that we were able to make and present something for Demo Day. If only we had more time, we would like to add more quests and even monsters to spice up the gameplay.
