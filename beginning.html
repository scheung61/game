No Ideas to Big Ideas

And Ideas that are improbable in 10+ hours.




At the very beginning, we had an idea of making a game similiar to T-Rex Run. The game is essentially an endless jumping game that pops up when there's no interent or wifi. In the beginning, we had no idea where to start and ended up giving up on building the game. However, hope was not lost when one of our team members found an engine that allows someone to create their own RPG game. We were all inspired and motivated to create something that we have never made before. Our creation of Springlight reflects all of our hard work and dedication and we are very proud of the RPG game we made together.
