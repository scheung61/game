No Ideas to Big Ideas

And Ideas that are improbable in 10+ hours.




It all started when we were at Uber ATG. We were on our laptops thinking about ideas for out project. At first, we had the idea of replicating the popular T-Rex game. For those who never heard of this game, it's essentially a jumping game on the google chrome web browser and it usually pops up when you don't have access to wifi or the interent. Once we started planning on making the game, we realized that we have no clue where to start. Therefore, we went back to searching for alternative ideas for our project. One of our team members found a cool engine that allows anyone to create an RPG game. We all then realized what are progect is going to be.
